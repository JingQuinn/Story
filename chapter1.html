Chapter 1 - New Member for the family

After our sister's family moved out, the house suddently went very quite. Dave decided to get a dog, so we went online and got contact with the seller for German shepherd in town. I still remembered the day we went to picked the dog, they brought him to the living room, he looked so small and cute, even he was only three months old. We didn't looked at other puppy, straight way decided to bring him home. Dave had same German shepherd when he was only 7 years old, it called Max, he was a police retired dog, well trained, and always protected Dave. That's another reason we chosen German Shepherd, we also called him - Max.
