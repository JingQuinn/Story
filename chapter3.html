Chapter 3 -- Sickness

The day we got Max, we also got a small bag of dog food from the seller, and they told us to soak the food first, then fed it, and we can change to any food we want. First few days we used the same food, and we found out Max kept having diarrhea. We didn't know what to do, then Dave suddenly remembered one of our neighbor has same German shepherd, so he went and asked for help.

We never talked to her before, just knew that she owned few German shepherd. Edwina is really nice lady, special for the first time to meet stranger like us, she spent lots time to ask us questions and told us what to do. From her knowledge, we found out the food that seller used to fed Max which called Red Mill, it is not good food for puppy, they used it because the cheaper price. She also told us that German shepherd's stomach is very sensitive, we need to change food immediately, but before started new food, she suggested us to feed Max with chicken mixed with rice for few days, and check Max's poo every day. If the poo became solid, then we can add in the new dog food, but do not change straight way, have to add in slowly until dog food totally instead chicken and rice.

We did what she said, Max's stiuation turned to normal, and we changed to better food as well. Because it happened in Max's puppy time, his stomach was sensitive than other dog. After we moved house, we all enjoyed in the new place, it close to hill of tara, so we can bring Max to a long walk, or we can just go to the field and walk with him. Dave trained him very well and he grew so big, everyone love Max. Also he did good job to protect us and house, every time if stranges come close to the door, he will bark and warned us. He is very smart and really became member of our family.
