Chapter 2 -- Puppy period

That winter was the coldest winter in Ireland. Since we had Max, one of us need to get up early in the morning, fed him, put fire on in the sitting room, and played with him. If he felt tired, then we can all slept bit before go to work. We need to lock all room's doors, only left kitchen and hall for him, leave toy and enough water for him when we away.

As a puppy, he need company, when we away in work, he must bored, one day Dave came home and found he chewed corner of the wall, and he continued doing it. Finally dave had to buy a bag of plaster to fix the wall, he left in the hall and planned to do it in the weekend. But the next day, when he came back, he opened the door and what a surprise for him. Max opened the plaster, and played with it, so the whole hall and kitchen's floor were full of plaster. Dave was angry, then he felt very funny. He took a picture and filmed it, then started cleaning.

Training is very important, special for German Shepherd, you need make their mind busy, and let them learning the commands since puppy. I don't have any experience, so Dave was training him, I just followed him and used same commands. People said there are only one boss for each dog, in our family Dave was the boss for Max, special his is the male dog. Max learned very quick, and he listened to Dave more than me. So in his puppy period, he loved to steal my stuff and played with it, such as my bag, hair dryer etc. But he never touch anything belonged to Dave.
